About Me

Here comes the story:

My father used to work in a foreign company and he deeply understands the importance of language at work. As the result, he emphasized in language training for my education since I was young. In my second year of highschool, I went to study abroad in the United States for a total of 8 years under my parents’ support. Other than improving my English skill, I also gained independent thinking and self-problem solving ability throughout this period. During my studies at UCLA, I had academic training about IC design, signal processing, semiconductor theories and C++ programing. These trainings became my foundation in the semiconductor and electronics industry. After I graduated from college, since I have always wanted to learn a second foreign language and plus my intention to gain more self competitiveness in Asia, I went to Tokyo to study Japanese for around half year and passed JLPT N2 level after it.
